# Student E-commerce Marketplace — 0→1 Product Build

## Overview

Built a student-facing D2C e-commerce marketplace for school essentials including books, uniforms, stationery, and accessories. The platform was designed to improve parent convenience, create a scalable digital commerce channel, and open a new B2C revenue stream.

## Problem Statement

Parents were dependent on offline and fragmented purchase journeys for school essentials. Institutions lacked a centralized commerce system for product discovery, ordering, fulfilment, and repeat purchases.

## My Role

As Product Manager, I owned the roadmap, product requirements, storefront configuration, discovery logic, PLP/PDP optimization, cross-sell strategy, and go-to-market execution.

## Key Responsibilities

- Defined the product roadmap for the e-commerce marketplace
- Created PRDs, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Designed product discovery flows from listing page to checkout
- Owned PLP and PDP optimization
- Defined product taxonomy, filters, tags, collections, and metadata
- Built cross-sell and recommendation logic
- Worked with engineering, UX, business, and operations teams
- Tracked GMV, AOV, cart abandonment, CLTV, and repeat purchase rate
- Ran A/B tests on key conversion points

## Product Features

- Product Listing Pages
- Product Detail Pages
- Product Taxonomy
- Smart Filters
- Cart and Checkout
- Cross-sell Recommendations
- Seasonal Bundles
- Order Fulfilment Flow

## Business Impact

- Increased parent engagement by 32%
- Reduced cart abandonment by 24%
- Improved browse-to-buy conversion by 19%
- Achieved 18% repeat purchase rate
- Created a new B2C revenue stream
