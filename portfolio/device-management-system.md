# Device Management System

## Overview

Designed a centralized Device Management System with real-time asset tracking, geo-fencing, remote intervention controls, policy enforcement, and compliance reporting workflows.

## Problem Statement

Institutions needed better visibility and control over distributed devices. Manual tracking created operational misuse, compliance gaps, and limited ability to intervene remotely.

## My Role

As Product Manager, I defined product requirements, wrote epics and user stories, managed Agile workflows, and validated requirements through UAT cycles with institutional clients.

## Key Responsibilities

- Designed device enrolment workflows
- Defined policy enforcement flows
- Created remote wipe and remote intervention requirements
- Built compliance reporting workflows
- Managed sprint planning, backlog grooming, estimation, and retrospectives
- Conducted UAT cycles with 5 institutional clients
- Coordinated with engineering, business, and operations teams

## Product Features

- Device Enrolment
- Real-time Asset Tracking
- Geo-fencing
- Remote Intervention
- Remote Wipe
- Policy Enforcement
- Compliance Reporting
- Admin Dashboard
- Device Usage Monitoring

## Business Impact

- Improved compliance monitoring
- Reduced operational misuse
- Improved sprint predictability by 20%
- Validated product workflows with 5 institutional clients
